Wine Analysis

Employed ridge and Lasso regression, decision tree, random forest, SVM and KNN to discover the relationship between physicochemical characteristics including acidity, residual sugar, density, pH and alcohol of white wine ‘Vino Verde’ and its sensory performance, which is an ordinal score from 1 to 10 evaluated by wine experts. Imputed missing values with Gaussian Copula model, standardized features with feature scaling, applied 5-folds cross validation and grid search for hyperparameter tuning.
